Diamondback Golf Club

Rating: 4.3/5

Phone number: (905) 888-9612

Location: 13300 Leslie St, Richmond Hill ON L4E 1A2

Course website: Diamondback Golf Club

General description: Since 1993, Diamondback Golf Club is a Clublink golf course located in the GTA North central area. Designed by Rene Muylaert, this 18-hole golf facility is composed of many hills, valleys, and daunting water hazards to carry. The front nine's openness contrasts the tightly packed back nine that is surrounded by tall trees and dense thickets. Diamondback has an adequate practice facility comprised of a driving range with coloured targets, and a large putting and chipping green.

Review: As one of Clublink's platinum level courses, Diamondback Golf Club is kept in exceptional shape with fast greens and even fairways. The holes are average in length, and the bunkers are nicely maintained throughout the course. Fairway and greenside bunkers are a common feature of Diamondback's holes, as well as the occasional water hazard stationed right before the green. A few of these water hazards require you to carry the distance in order to finish the hole, which offers a refreshing challenge on your distance control. The island green 8th and the 18th were the most memorable holes of the course, as they provided an excellent finish to the front nine and the back nine respectively.

What stood out to me was the contrasting layouts of the front nine and the back nine; the first half of my round at Diamondback transitioned into an entirely different experience going into the back nine. The uniqueness of the course made my round enjoyable and entertaining, as it was full of twist and turns and risky challenges. The consistency of the greens may vary at times, with some holes feeling softer than other holes, though the course is generally deemed to be fast-rolling.
