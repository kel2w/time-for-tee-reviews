Hylands Golf Club - South

Rating: 3.9/5

Phone number: (613) 521-1842

Location: 2101 Alert Rd, Gloucester, ON K1V 1J9

Course website: Hylands Golf and Country Club

General description: Hylands Golf Club is a private golf facility located near the Ottawa Internation Airport in Ottawa, ON, with the purpose of providing affordable golf and services to military forces. The South Course began its development in 1961, while plans to construct the North Course commenced in 1969. Hylands Golf Club officially opened in 1972, resembling a Parkland-style golf course, as each hole is divided by an abundance of trees.

Review: I played at Hylands in the fall, and the course was in good shape despite the windy weather scattering leaves all over. I would say the biggest obstacle is getting your shot out of the trees from a bad slice or hook. There were no water hazards on the entire course except for Hylands' scenic 18th hole, which featured a large pond fountain just before the green. If you opt to hit longer, you may find the bunker in the back — No. 18 is considered to be the most challenging hole on the South Course, with emphasis on making the right club selection and committing to the shot for a good score.

The South Course's putting greens were well-kept, but they were considerably larger than the green sizes of other courses in the region, especially its par 3s. Around the greens, there were tidy, compact bunkers that are often positioned on the front left side. From the tee, the line of trees on both sides of the fairway produced a narrow look, and many holes sharing the same linear layout with a few trees on the side as an obstacle made the golfing experience somewhat repetitive. The simplicity of this golf course does not offer much excitement in a round, however, it is a good quality course for the average golfer.
