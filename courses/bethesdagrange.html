Bethesda Grange Golf Course at The Rolling Hills Golf Club

Rating: 3.5/5

Phone number: (905) 888-1955

Location: 12808 Warden Ave., Whitchurch-Stouffville, ON L4A 7X5

Course website: Rolling Hills Golf Club

General description: The Rolling Hills Golf Club is a 36-hole golfing facility with a par-70 Challenge Course, a par-62 Classic Course, and a par-72 Championship Course. However, the Championship Course went under renovation, and was renamed as the Bethesda Grange Golf Course at The Rolling Hills Golf Club. This Clublink golf club is ranked at the Silver Level by Clublink's standards, and it takes on a simpler version of the Parkland-style course type. For its amenities, Rolling Hills provides a practice range, a medium-sized putting green, and a bistro for food and dining services during and after the round.

Review: Bethesda Grange Golf Course offers a satisfactory golfing experience at an affordable rate, with its comfortably walkable design, and throwing in a mixture of hills, creeks, lakes, and trees to add a variety of challenges to the golf course. The overall quality of the course was average at best due to uneven greens, patchy fairways, and poorly maintained hazards. There were stumps and broken tree branches grouped in several areas of the course, and many untrimmed hazards that contributed to a shabby appearance.

Bethesda Grange can get crowded at times, resulting in a slow pace of play with long waits between shots. This is due to the Rolling Hill Golf Club's popularity among casual golfers, as it is recognized for its affordability and beginner-friendly course layout. If you aren't looking to splurge on an extravagant golfing experience or if you are just starting out in golf, Bethesda Grange is the perfect place to practice your skills on the golf course.
